Why should I join?

If you ever wanted to make a game but can't figure out how, you can create a game whether it be with your friends, by yourself, or meet other people and make a new team. And if you are feeling reluctant you can still give it a go.

What can I do? Do I need experience?

If you have prior experience that's great, feel free to show us your portfolio. However you don't need experience to join. Game design is broad, including roles such as programming & coding , digital art & animation, music & soundtrack composing, and level design & brainstorming.

We create games in teams to compete in events such as gamejams. Game jams are competitions where you create a game in a given timeframe inspired by a theme. Check out gamejams at Itch.io.

You can also work on your own projects that aren't involved in gamejams, whether solo or with a team. This game design club is just here to share ideas, give feedback, and help find those nasty bugs in your game If you encounter any and can't figure out how to solve them.
